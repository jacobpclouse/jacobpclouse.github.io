My Meditation App

A responsive, modern application

Why I Built This:

I wanted to apply the modern Javascript I was learning. I found a great video by DevEd who went through this clever design.

Challenges Faced:

Setting up the overlays to match up correctly. Changing the times of the specific time presets wasn't difficult, but keeping the CSS Dial in line was. I needed to center it correctly otherwise it would be confusing to the user.

Lessons Learned:

It was very interesting to see how Javascript could be used to tackle projects that I couldn't even imagine. The elegance of the implimentation is very efficient. It made me realize that the only limit to the language is one's imagination!
